HOW IT WORKS?

When you step on Pavetra tiles, kinetic energy triggers a free-fall screw and cycloidal gearbox. This motion is converted into clean electricity through an internal actuator housing.

Pitch-Flow Screw

Advanced free-fall screw mechanism.⁠Enables rapid and controlled descent with minimal resistance.

Cycloidal Gear Box

Precisely-engineered 1:10 wobble gear system. ⁠Delivers efficient torque transmission with minimal backlash.

Internal Cycloidal Actuator Housing

⁠Optimized internal casing design. ⁠Houses coils and magnets for seamless energy conversion.

Enhanced Power Production

⁠Integrated system ensures maximum energy output. ⁠Converts kinetic energy from footsteps into sustainable electricity.
